Interests

Aviation

Fascinated by the technological achievements and precision of the aviation industry, I pursue this interest through flight simulation. This activity, when practiced in a group setting, has allowed me to develop strong teamwork skills, precise communication during formation flights, as well as excellent stress management and enhanced responsiveness in complex situations.

Space

Space exploration and astronomy are constant sources of inspiration for me. The complexity of these fields fuels my curiosity and my passion for research and innovation. This passion for the unknown and for large-scale technological challenges strengthens my ability to understand complex system architectures and devise solutions.

Video games

The world of video games, and more specifically, modding and dedicated server administration, serves as a true laboratory for technical experimentation for me. Setting up complex multiplayer environments, optimizing resources, and providing technical support for a group of users have allowed me to develop solid practical skills in system administration, networking, and technical troubleshooting.
